> cat intro.txt

CYPH3R is the official Cybersecurity and Technology Club of IIIT Kota, led by a community of passionate students dedicated to exploring, learning, and innovating in the field of cybersecurity. The club's mission is to build a collaborative cybersecurity culture within the institute by promoting knowledge sharing, hands-on learning, and responsible digital practices.

CYPH3R regularly conducts workshops, technical sessions, and Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions to provide practical exposure to topics such as networking, web security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. In addition to technical training, the club organizes interactive and engaging events such as Byte Break and other cybersecurity-themed activities to make learning more enjoyable and accessible.

By combining education, innovation, and collaboration, CYPH3R aims to empower students to develop the skills, curiosity, and confidence needed to excel in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity and technology.
